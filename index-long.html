Featured Projects.

ramble
Empowering Women-Identifying Individuals to Walk Alone Safely

An application that supports individual walkers while tackling walking safety as a society. Ramble connects walkers with a community of allies and educational resources on walking safety. Passive data collection during walks provides city planners with meaningful information, enabling walkers to impact their environments.

Video Turbine
Making Product Video Production more Accessible to Businesses

A web-based video production service that enables businesses to design and order their own videos by choosing from a tailored selection of options. Think Squarespace for video production.

dataLab
Connecting Businesses to their Live Data through Shareable Dashboards

A Business Intelligence (BI) desktop application that keeps businesses informed of critical performance metrics through live-updating D3 charts on customizable, shareable dashboards.

canvas
Increasing Student Engagement in a Learning Managment Platform

A redesign of Canvas to facilitate more engaging learning experiences by making assignments shareable, encouraging reflection after assignments have been turned in and making assigned readings interactive, augmenting communication in online discussion forums.
