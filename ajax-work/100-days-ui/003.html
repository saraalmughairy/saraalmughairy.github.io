003. Landing Page (Above the Fold)

What's the main focus? Is it for a book, an album, a mobile app, a product? Consider important landing page elements (call-to-actions, clarity, etc.)

For this exercise, I wanted to explore a dramatic shift from a dark interface to a white one. Borrowing from my experience with cinematography, I decided to simulate the effect of panning a camera across a light source, which results in a lens flare. By combining animations created in Principle with lighting in After Effects, this splash screen embraces its video production content with a cinematic theme. Once the user hits the landing page, we want them to get drawn into the video-building process, so the call to action seemed obvious.

Tools used: Sketch, Principle, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Photoshop
