008. User Profile

Design a user profile and be mindful of the most important data, names, imagery, placement, etc.

I've had an idea for an app that connects tattooists to customers for a while. I see this as being a platform where ink enthusiasts can browse for tattoos and tattooists that inspire them (kind of like a combo of Instagram,Pinterest, and Yelp but with tattoo-centric features and information architecture) and book consultations and appointments with tattooists when they are ready to get a tattoo. For this challenge, I chose to mock up a profile page for a tattooist. There are tags that show the genre of tattoos that tattooist specializes in (e.g., American Traditional), examples of the tattooist's work, and reviews from previous clients.

Tools used: Sketch, Adobe Photoshop
