005. App Icon

Design an app icon. What best represents the brand or product? Or is it incredibly unique? Does it look great at a distance and does it stand out when put on your home screen alongside other apps?

For this challenge, I wanted to blend photorealistic depth with the flat design popular among ios apps. I chose a record player because it is recognizable from an aerial perspective, and can naturally take a square shape (like an ios app icon).

Tools used: Adobe Illustrator
