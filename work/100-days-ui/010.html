010. Social Sharing

Design a social share button/icon and be mindful of the size, imagery, placement, and purpose for sharing.

My goal was to make a fun and lively interaction for sharing. To achieve this feel, I added some spring to the animation when the social icons fly out and a lil' spin to the sharing icon. When the user makes the decision to share content, that is glorious win for the content producer and as such, this behavior should be encouraged through an engaging interaction.

Tools used: Sketch, Principle, Adobe PS
