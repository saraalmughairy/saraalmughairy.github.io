002. Credit Card Checkout

Design a credit card checkout form or page. Don't forget the important elements such as the numbers, dates, security numbers, etc.

For this payment screen challenge, I decided to focus on ease of use. By designing the form to resemble a physical debit card and by using calm, friendly gradients and modern minimalist aesthetics, this interface aims to make the payment process as simple and stylish as possible.

Tools used: Sketch, Principle, Adobe Photoshop
