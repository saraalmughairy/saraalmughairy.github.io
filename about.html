design philosophy.

Nothing exists in a vaccuum.

My interdisciplinary background has led me to be a systems thinker, meaning I appreciate that there are direct and indirect use cases and stakeholders to consider when designing for even seemingly small interactions. Operating under this framework enables me to design with intention.

ship early & often

Rapid iteration and user feedback are key. As painful as it is to release something with flaws, a strong design process extends outside the office bubble.

give back to your community

Whether it's mentoring other WOC in tech or contributing to community projects, working with and empathisizing with others only strengthens my design approach and I admire companies who promote this mentality internally.
